Fight the Aliens Along Side the Hobo

More alien police come and surround the both of you. You kill a few but are overpowered, they cuff you and you black out. You wake up in a hospital, IV's connected to your arms. A nurse notices your awake and explains that the hobo laced the sunglasses with liquid LSD, causing you to go into a drug rage.
